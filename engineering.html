RC Car With Video Streaming

For my 19th birthday party, I organized a building party to construct an RC Car with a Raspberry Pi that could stream the output of the attached camera. We connected to it through WiFi, so that we were could drive it around without even being in the room! We saw the world all from the car's perspective, just like a video game!
