RC Car With Video Streaming

For my 19th birthday party, I organized a building party where my friends an I constructed an RC Car with a Raspberry Pi that could stream the output of the attached camera.

I configured its pins to be controllable over WiFi. This way, we were able to drive it around without even being in the room, all from the car's perspective!
